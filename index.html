Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Red Fort

Emperor Shah Jahan commissioned the construction of the Red Fort on 12 May 1639, following his decision to relocate the Mughal capital from Agra to Delhi.

Qutub Minar

The Qutb Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Mehrauli area of South Delhi, India.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an arch-monument completed in 1924 on the waterfront of Mumbai (Bombay), India.

Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple is a 13th-century Hindu temple in Odisha, India, dedicated to the sun god Surya.
